Curriculum Inspirations is a video series starring James Tanton that was conceptualized and produced while Laura was working at the Mathematical Association of America. In each video, James use his ten established strategies to break down complex problems from MAA's American Mathematics Competitions exams. In addition to shooting and editing over 160 videos for this series, Laura also managed marketing this series to MAA members and the greater math community. A full playlist is available here
